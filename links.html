Baliga lab at ISB

The Baliga Lab reverse engineers biological circuits to understand how cells adapt to new environments. The lab's long term vision is to use predictive mathematical gene regulatory network models to drive engineering designer circuits for a variety of biotechnological applications such as bioenergy, bioremediation and medicine.

Institute for Systems Biology

ISB pioneered systems-approaches to biology. Since its founding in 2000, the ISB has been a global leader in personalized medicine, environmental sustainability, and global health research. In addition to its strong advocacy for open and interdisciplinary science, the Institute has been recognized for its contribution to modernizing scientific education paradigms.
